Screw Compressors

Screw Compressors are highly efficient air compressors widely used in industries for continuous and reliable compressed air supply. They use two rotating helical screws (rotors) to compress air, ensuring smooth operation, low vibration, and high energy efficiency.

High Efficiency & Energy Saving
24/7 Continuous Duty Operation
Low Noise & Vibration
Low Maintenance Requirement
Compact & Space-Saving Design
Stable Air Pressure Output

Applications

Manufacturing Industries
Automobile Workshops
Pharmaceutical Plants
Food & Beverage Processing
Textile & Packaging
Construction & Engineering

Advantages

  • Stable air pressure output
  • Longer service life
  • Lower operating cost
  • Better performance vs. piston compressors

Reciprocating Compressors

Reciprocating (Piston) Compressors are heavy-duty, proven technology compressors that use pistons driven by a crankshaft to deliver high-pressure compressed air. Ideal for intermittent use and high-pressure applications across diverse industries.

High-Pressure Capability
Robust & Heavy-Duty Build
Wide Range of Capacities
Easy Field Maintenance
Air / Water Cooled Options
Cost-Effective Solution

Applications

Oil & Gas Industry
Construction Sites
Paint & Spray Systems
Chemical Plants
Marine & Offshore
General Industries

Advantages

  • Very high discharge pressures
  • Excellent for intermittent use
  • Proven & reliable technology
  • Lower upfront investment
